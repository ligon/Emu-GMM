* Phase 7 design brief (#42 gauge-invariant Gamma-functional SEs)
*Problem.* The Phase-4 minimal =Sigma_theta= is the ambient-horizontal =pinv_eigvalrule=
sandwich over the tangent θ=(Y,phi). Its raw per-entry Y SEs are gauge-ARBITRARY (any
Y in the O(K) orbit gives a different Y but the same Gamma=AAᵀ). The consumer (K-Aggregators)
needs interpretable SEs of *functionals of Gamma* (eigenvalues, entries) and of phi.

*Method (delta method, gauge-invariant by construction).* For a functional
=f(θ) = g(Gamma(θ), phi)= that depends on θ ONLY through gauge-invariants (Gamma=AAᵀ and phi):
- =J_f = ∂f/∂θ= at the fixed =theta_hat= (AD of =f∘unflatten= on the flat ambient θ;
  commitment 5 — at fixed theta_hat, NOT through the solver).
- =Cov(f) = J_f Sigma_theta J_f'= ; =SE(f) = sqrt(diag)=.
- Gauge-invariance is automatic: a gauge-invariant =f= has =J_f= orthogonal to the O(K)
  gauge fibre, so it annihilates the exact gauge nullspace already pinned out of
  =Sigma_theta=; the result is identical for Y0 and Y0·Q. (A gauge-VIOLATING f gets nonzero
  variance from the gauge directions — used as a negative control.)

*API (proposed; conveniences over one general primitive).*
- =result.functional_se(f)= — general: =f(components)->R^p= returns =(se_vector, covariance)=.
- =result.gamma_se()= — SE of each Gamma entry (vech(Gamma)); =result.gamma_covariance()=.
- =result.eigenvalue_se()= — SE of =eigvalsh(Gamma)= (the K-Agg primary).
The conveniences are thin wrappers over =functional_se=. v1/scalar trees: reduces to the
ordinary delta-method SE (e.g. SE of a scalar transform), bitwise where it overlaps.

*Validation.* (a) gauge-invariance: Y0 vs Y0·Q give identical functional SEs (the core
guarantee); (b) correctness: delta-method eigenvalue/entry SEs match a parametric-bootstrap
(or finite-difference) reference on a synthetic DGP to MC tolerance; (c) negative control:
a gauge-violating functional shows nonzero variance / is rejected; (d) v1 non-regression.
*Out of scope / optional:* =spectral_gap= diagnostic, the high-gauge-fraction (k/n>0.7)
regime (K-Agg stays k/n=0.4–0.6 — surface a caveat, don't support it).
